About Me

Hello! My name is William.


I graduated from the University of Tulsa with degrees in electrical engineering and engineering physics, and I have had 4 years of experience in flight simulation. I am currently the lab manager and an electrical engineering instructor at the University of Tulsa. I grew up on a farm and enjoy welding and building things.




Values

Privacy and security are of the utmost importance to me. Though it is impossible to guarantee, I believe it is important to do the best we can. This website collects zero information and uses no external content. I strive to use the least amount of personal data as possible. Any data that must be collected is kept under encrypted local storage.

Keeping it simple is often the most elegant solution. If something is very labor-intensive compared to the cost of parts perhaps it's time to re-evaluate the problem.

Usability is one of the most underrated aspects of engineering. If you have ever tried to push a pull door, then you know what I am talking about, and it's not your fault! Often the psychology of how the mechanics meet the person is completely overlooked. I aim to eliminate as many of these obstacles as possible.
